Stop learning and start building!

Novice programmers think they need to attend all the workshops, take all the online courses, and spend days on simple projects to become a good enough programmer. Truth is, today, you don't need to be at that level to make an impact on the world and affect millions of lives. You just need to be creative and determined to fix things.

Coders on the Road is a team of programmers, of all levels, travelling around the world to build meaningful solutions that have a massive impact on those who need them. From the African farmers suffering from droughts to tackling homelessness in London, we are on a mission to develop one standalone project every month.

Our approach: Get in, fix the the problem, make the solution sustainable, get out.
